How To Prepare For a Home
Solar System?

Energy Tracking

Track the amount of energy you have been using at home in the past few months. This will give you an idea for the solar output you will require.

Roofing Space

Adequate roof space is crucial for the optimal performance of residential solar panels. This ensures the solar panels are installed to maximise sunlight absorption, allowing you to harness as much solar energy as possible to benefit your home and family.
